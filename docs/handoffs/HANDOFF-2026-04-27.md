### The structural bottleneck this session

The test period for 365d lookback covers ~Nov 2025 – Apr 2026:
- DeepSeek shock: January 2026
- Trump tariff shock: April 2026

Most momentum strategies fail in this test window. The 10 validated symbols are exceptional in
having strong edge that survived BOTH macro shocks. Adding more symbols requires either:
- Longer lookback (730d) to shift the training/test split to a more favorable window
- Time filter (open-only or trend+open) to concentrate to the highest signal-to-noise window
- Custom lookback (500d for TGT) to find the optimal split for each symbol
