## Key insight discovered: behavior classifier miscalibration

`behavior=apply` classifies most of our profitable symbols as non-`neutral`, blocking 95% of signals.
The `confirmationRequired` profile (38.46% WR, +$1,114 in backtest) is the dominant profitable profile
but gets blocked in apply mode. The fix: use `behavior=observe` + explicit `--behavior-filter` whitelist.

**Do not flip back to `behavior=apply`** without first mapping which profiles it allows/blocks.
