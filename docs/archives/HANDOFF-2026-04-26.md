- `LIVE_TRADING_MAX_NOTIONAL` — per-order notional cap (default $250)
- `LIVE_TRADING_ALLOWED_SYMBOLS` — comma-separated allowlist
- `LIVE_TRADING_REQUIRE_PROTECTIVE_STOP` — reject live buys without stopPrice (default true)
